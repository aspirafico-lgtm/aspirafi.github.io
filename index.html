FAQ

Questions we get all the time

Everything you need to know before applying.

Is collateral required for education loans?

Collateral is not mandatory for Masters courses. Our lender partners can provide non-collateral loans ranging from ₹80 lakh to ₹1 crore based on your profile, university, and course.

Can I apply before receiving my admission letter?

Yes, you can start the eligibility check and pre-application process before your official admission letter arrives. A conditional sanction letter will be provided, which can strengthen your visa application significantly.

Does checking eligibility affect my credit score?

Banks will do a hard credit check at the time of formal application. But before that, we do a detailed profile assessment to shortlist only the lenders most likely to approve you. This way you avoid unnecessary hard checks and protect your credit score.

Which countries and universities are covered?

We cover USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Ireland, Netherlands, and New Zealand. Most accredited universities in these countries qualify. If you're unsure about your university, just apply and we'll confirm within 24 hours.

What is the repayment period and moratorium?

Most lenders offer a moratorium (no EMI) for the course duration + 6–12 months after you finish. Repayment tenure is 7–15 years depending on the lender and loan amount.

Is Aspirafi's service free? How do you make money?

Yes, completely free for students. We earn a small referral fee from the lender when your loan is disbursed. This does not affect your interest rate or loan terms in any way.
